How to Navigate the Website

Use the navigation bar at the top of each page to visit different parts of the website like Home, Gallery, Booking, and more.

Booking Form Instructions

To book an appointment, go to the Booking page, fill all the fields such as name, department, gender, and comments, then click Submit.

Viewing Gallery & Data

In the Gallery page, you can see hospital photos and patient data. Click any image to view it in detail.

Frequently Asked Questions
